Frequently Asked Questions

How do I adopt a pet?

To adopt a pet, browse our pet adoption feed, find your perfect match, and click on "Request Adoption". Fill out the adoption form and our team will contact you within 24 hours.

How do I book a vet appointment?

You can book a vet appointment through our website by clicking on "Vet Appointment" in the navigation bar. Select your preferred vet, date, and time slot.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, mobile banking, and cash on delivery for our services.

How can I join the pet community?

Simply create an account on PawConnect and click on "Community" to join discussions, share photos, and connect with other pet owners.
